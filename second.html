Meesho

Meesho is an Indian e-commerce marketplace founded in 2015 by Vidit Aatrey and Sanjeev Barnwal, focused on providing low-cost products to consumers and enabling individuals and small businesses to sell online. It operates as an asset-light platform, connecting sellers, customers, and logistics providers with a zero-commission model for sellers to help them offer competitive prices.
